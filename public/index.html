💭 Social Proof

A psychological phenomenon where people try to assume the actions of others in an attempt to correct the behavior for a given situation. Social proof affects us in almost all aspects of our life, and its repercussions could be as mild as laughing at something thats not funny or as drastic as drug addiction.
